# Draft 1

6 days nigh had passed  
not a raindrop fell. 
12 men walked by  
each ringing my bell. 
A thirteenth man  
crossed over from Hell. 
A house-broken dog  
lay alone in the Dell. 
And crestfallen,
I passed time alone in my head. 
6 days now had passed  
not a raindrop would fall.
